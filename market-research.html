03 · Gaps & Opportunities

Where the Growth Is

Most of these gaps are quick, structural fixes — and every one of them compounds the others once the plumbing works.

01

Fix the Broken Funnel First

Dead Instagram link on both of Saymara's sites, a dead Facebook page linked from treromania.org, a dead community invite, an empty Twitter icon. Day-one fixes that stop losing every referred visitor.

02

Unify the Rebrand

Two parallel Facebook presences (4.2K profile + 2.8K page), a TikTok on the old name, and three websites. One handle set, one visual identity, clear roles per property, and cross-links between both brands' sites and socials.

03

Give Instagram a Distribution Strategy

453 posts have earned just 233 followers — the content exists, the reach doesn't. Reels formats, hashtags, collabs with the 29-facilitator network, and cross-brand tagging change that fast.

04

Own Romanian-Language Video

Saymara's YouTube is dormant and TRE® România's "channel" belongs to a third party. One owned, Romanian-language video program — explaining neurogenic tremor, client stories, workshop clips — fills a niche nobody in Romania owns.

05

Turn the Events Engine into Content

41 events in two years, Berceli retreats, a book, free demos — and a site calendar that currently shows nothing upcoming. Systematic event cross-posting alone gives both brands a steady content rhythm.

06

Claim LinkedIn & Corporate Wellness

Saymara's reactivated LinkedIn B2B positioning pairs naturally with a TRE® România organisation page targeting corporate stress-management — an empty, high-value lane in Bucharest's corporate market.

05 · Where to Start

The Priority Moves

1

Repair every broken link

Fix the dead Instagram link, the dead TRE® Facebook link, the dead community invite and the empty Twitter icon — the cheapest, highest-impact fixes available.

2

Unify handles & cross-link both brands

One name — Saymara B. Ryon — everywhere; TikTok and legacy pages migrated; both websites and every profile pointing to each other.

3

Start a steady two-brand content rhythm

A shared calendar: every workshop and demo cross-posted to page, group, Instagram and LinkedIn — with reels formats that finally give the content distribution.

4

Build the owned video channel + B2B lane

A Romanian-language YouTube/Reels program on TRE® and conscious living, plus a TRE® România LinkedIn page for corporate wellness.

The practice and the community already exist. The job is to make sure the right people can find them online — see the showcase for what that looks like.
